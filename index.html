About Chowki

Nature is generous for Assam by providing amazing natural beauty. And, due to lack of rebellion, people are making their way to various tourist destinations across the state.
CHOWKI, a picnic spot at Uttarkuci in Baksa district of Assam, is attracting hundreds of people from across the state. Distance from Guwahati to Chowki picnic spot 120.7 km (3 hour 2 min) via NH27.
Huge natural beauty, filled with green mountains and roaming rivers, which crosses its diverse landscape, this place cures picnics. Minimum admission fee is taken from each picnic party and collected revenue is used for the maintenance of the place. Located at the Indo- Bhutan Border, CHOWKI was once affected by militant activity, but But as normalcy returns, so do tourists.
